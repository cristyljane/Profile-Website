My Projects

Application Development

As part of my coursework, I developed an innovative recipe app designed to make cooking and meal planning easier for users. The app allows users to search for recipes and upload their own. With a user-friendly interface and a focus on convenience, the app simplifies the process of discovering, sharing, and organizing recipes, bringing a culinary experience to life at your fingertips.

Circuit Making in Logic Subject

In our Logic subject, We worked on creating circuits using breadboards and logic gates. This project was a hands-on experience that helped us understand the practical applications of logic theory.

Point of Sale (POS) System

In this project, I developed a Point of Sale (POS) system, designed to streamline business transactions. The system included inventory management and transaction tracking features.

Multimedia Projects

Throughout my studies, I have worked on various multimedia projects, which included graphic design, video editing, and animation. These projects allowed me to creatively express ideas and enhance my technical skills.
